Chess From Trash

Active

The project we chose is called Chess from Trash and you’re supposed to create a game of chess from trash or from recycled plastic turned into filament to 3d print for use in space. Here are some restraints (btw my team is going the 3d printed route with recycled trash for the storing of the board and pieces) * The board has to be 12x12 inches or close enough. * no height requirements * We are limited to a 6x6 inch platform for printing * We must find a way to assemble the board and attach ch pieces * Our chess game must have a creative theme * Our pieces have to be modeled to fit into that theme * We cannot use Velcro, magnets, or any liquid/viscus adhesive for safety purposes -must have a tile pattern and pieces with 2 colors

I am the lead designer and CAD modeler

Started October 2025
Status In progress
Tools Im using multiple CAD's for this project as they have different strenghts, due to working on a chromebook. OnShape is very accessible and easy to use. Solidworks is has more features but takes long to load due to using a virtual machine. Fusion 360 has a lot of features, including T-spline modeling, and I can access it online. We're printing in a regular Creality Ender-3, using SimplyPrint for Orca and Prusa sclicers. SelfCAD slicer is free but less versatile.
