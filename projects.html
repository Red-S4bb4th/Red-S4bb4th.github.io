Chess From Trash

Active

The project is called Chess from Trash and you’re supposed to create a game of chess from trash or from recycled plastic turned into filament to 3d print. Here are some restraints

  • The board has to be 12x12 inches or close enough.
  • no height requirements
  • We must find a way to assemble the board and attach chess pieces
  • Our chess game must have a creative theme
  • Our pieces have to be modeled to fit into that theme
  • We cannot use Velcro, magnets, or any liquid/viscus adhesive for safety purposes -must have a tile pattern and pieces with 2 colors

I am the lead designer and CAD modeler

Started October 2025
Status In progress
Tools Im using multiple CAD's for this project as they have different strenghts, due to working on a chromebook. OnShape is very accessible and easy to use. Solidworks is has more features but takes long to load due to using a virtual machine. Fusion 360 has a lot of features, including T-spline modeling, and I can access it online. We're printing in a regular Creality Ender-3, using SimplyPrint for Orca and Prusa sclicers. SelfCAD slicer is free but less versatile. View DevLog →
